Welcome to BotBuilders Academy

Explore the magic of robotics and coding in a fun and accessible way

At BotBuilders Academy, we believe every child has the potential to become a technology creator. Our online platform offers dynamic courses and interactive activities designed to spark interest in robotics and coding from an early age.

Dive into our beginner courses to learn the basics of robotics and coding while building simple and fascinating robots. Participate in interactive activities that let you explore the world of robots in a practical and entertaining way.

Join our online events to showcase your creations, celebrate your achievements, and connect with other young robotics enthusiasts. With a child-friendly design and intuitive navigation, BotBuilders Academy is the perfect place to start your journey into the STEM world.
